**Syntax**
`<>` is a required argument, [] is an optional argument

**Commands**
`help` - Displays this message.
`play/youtube <text/url>` - Plays a track from youtube.
`soundcloud <text/url>` - Plays a track from soundcloud. ~~broken~~
`np/nowplaying` - Shows what track is currently playing.
`queue` - Shows the current queue. ~~semi-functioning~~
`tts <text>` - Sends a text to speech message in the current VC.
`dj <@role/list>` - Sets a role as a DJ or shows which roles have DJ.
`ping` - Shows the bots ping.
