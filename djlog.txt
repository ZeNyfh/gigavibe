**Syntax**
`<>` is a required argument, [] is an optional argument

**Commands**
`play <text/url>` - Plays a track from youtube.
`skip` - Casts a vote to skip the current song.
`np` - Shows what song is now playing.
`tts <text>` - Makes the bot say something. (won't work if there is music playing)
`queue/q`- Sends the current music queue.
`clear queue` - Clears the music queue. (DJ only)
`forceskip/fs` - Skips a song forcibly. (DJ only)
